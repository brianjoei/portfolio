About

I'm a Product Designer with 3+ years of experience in UX and Interaction Design with a passion for prototyping and usability testing.


As a designer, I enjoy working on technology that helps people save time, allows exploration of the world, and that is simply fun to use. My background in front-end web development and social psychology uniquely allows me to rapidly build high fidelity prototypes and objectively validate concepts in usability test. Working through the full cycle of human centered design from user research to delivery is what I would like to work on next. Let's chat if you see good fit!


When I'm not designing, I'm riding my bike for ridiculous distances, cooking authentic Indonesian food, or attempting to get better at table tennis.
